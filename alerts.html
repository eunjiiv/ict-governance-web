Public Cybersecurity Advisories

🚨 How to Report Identity Theft

1. Contact the NBI Cybercrime Division immediately.

2. Change all passwords and enable 2FA.

3. Report via the DICT Cert-PH portal.

Detecting Fake Websites

  • Check for "HTTPS" in the URL.
  • Look for spelling errors in the domain name.
  • Avoid clicking links in unsolicited text messages (Smishing).
