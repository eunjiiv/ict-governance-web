Involved Government Agencies

DICT

Department of Information and Communications Technology

The primary body responsible for the planning and promotion of the country's ICT agenda.

  • Formulates national ICT policies.
  • Provides public access to digital services (Free Wi-Fi).

NPC

National Privacy Commission

The agency mandated to protect personal data privacy and implement the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

  • Handles data breach complaints.
  • Regulates personal data processing.

NBI - Cybercrime Division

Investigates cybercrimes like hacking, identity theft, and online scams in coordination with the DOJ.
